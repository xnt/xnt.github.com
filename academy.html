This page is a joint effort between the Nearsoft Academy to provide you resources during your internship, regarding Software Development topics and emphasizing in Java and related technologies.

If you're enrolled in the Nearsoft Academy, contact your mentor so he can assist you on how to use this page, based on your strenghts and opportunity points. Otherwise, read as you wish.

If you have a suggestion, or would like to see your book/post/app/whatever linked here, feel free to Contact Me or send a pull request to this repo.
